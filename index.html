Annual Get-Together

At PS Classes, we believe in fostering a strong and lasting bond among our students and faculty. Every year, after the 10th SSC board examinations, we organize a special get-together where students, teachers, and alumni gather to enjoy meals, engage in meaningful conversations, and celebrate their hard work and achievements. This cherished tradition helps build lifelong memories and a supportive community spirit among all members of PS Classes.

About Dr. Pooja Samant

Dr. Pooja Samant, a Ph.D. doctorate, has been passionately teaching Marathi and Hindi for over 15 years. She holds an M.A. in Marathi and is the author of three published books (with more coming soon). Her vast experience includes guest lectures at reputed schools, hosting and anchoring various educational events, and active participation in social work. Awarded multiple times for her contributions to education, she combines her love for teaching and communication to make language learning easy, engaging, and rewarding for students.
